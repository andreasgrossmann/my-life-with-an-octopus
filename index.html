My life with an octopus

Once upon a time in Lisbon, an octopus and a shark met.

They talked over wine and beer.

While they were apart, they messaged and talked a lot.

They met again in Vienna...

Where the octopus noticed that the shark is very meticulous.

"Look at the way I live, do you really think I separate my clothes?"

The shark receives his shark certificate for being very shark about... everything.

The octopus and the shark deal with a lot of paperwork to find an apartment and to get a visa for the octopus.

After a long struggle, which was almost too emotional - even for the shark - the octopus receives her visa and stays with the shark.
